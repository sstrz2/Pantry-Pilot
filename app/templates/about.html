Project Overview

Pantry Pilot is an initiative that partners with local universities to combat student hunger and food insecurity. Our innovative app creates a safety net for students in need, making it easy for them to request food assistance. A dedicated network of volunteers provides nutritious meals and access to campus food pantries, addresses accessibility challenges, reduces food waste, and promotes community engagement within educational institutions. The program allows students to focus on learning and thrive while ensuring that no student goes hungry. The program is designed to ensure that no student goes hungry, minimize food waste, strengthen community ties, and help students succeed academically.
